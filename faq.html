FAQ

What do I receive when i purchase a yearly Membership Fee to UWOP, and what does it allow me to do?

There is no membership fee, when you purchase any of our education and support packages, you will have access you automatically become a member.

Your membership will only be removed if you are not active for a year.

 

Do I have to refer people to UWOP to be eligible to receive any rewards potentially?

Yes, IF you want to receive Rewards through referral, BUT our educational packages and group page is more profitable than our reward plans when you refer others, it is optional for member to refer anyone it is not compulsory.

Does UWOP make any promises to anyone that invests in their education that they will guarantee financial success?

No, similar to paying for any educational courses like college courses or graduate courses, what you use the education is dependent on the individual. Your success and others' success is all dependent on how each individual learns and applies the knowledge they have learned.

Can the courses offered through UWOP packages be found anywhere by simply going to Google or YouTube?

No, not the exact but there are similar programs out there, but UWOP courses are unique to our Independent Associates and are not something other companies offer. If one does a Google or YouTube search, you can find some basic knowledge to start.

As an individual, I can sign up for multiple accounts to increase my opportunity to receive multiple revenue streams from the company?

No, an individual is allowed only one account to access our education. Signing up for multiple accounts is strictly prohibited and could result in immediate termination.

Can I purchase the UWOP packages with a credit card?

No, because UWOP is a digital education platform, and all purchases must be made in USDT.
